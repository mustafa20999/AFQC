Apple Fruit Quality Categorization (AFQC), has 2, 320 apples in total and consists of two classes: 1,160 fresh and 1,160 rotten apples were collected from the orchard.
China was the research region, Beijing City, Huairou District, place name 'Beijing Shengshiguowang', mean temperature '76°C - 19°C', mean monthly rainfall: 51.2 mm.
